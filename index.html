Take Macray is a Canadian singer, songwriter and dancer. She first gained fame as the first Canadian finalist of the American reality show “So You Think You Can Dance”. She later signed a contract with RCA Records after her original song "One Day" went viral on YouTube and TikTok, and she released two EPs, All the Things I Never Said and Too Young to Be Sad. In 2021, McRarey became the youngest musician to be included in the Forbes 30 Under 30. The last EP became the most downloaded women's EP mini-album of 2021 on Spotify, and its release was preceded by the single "You Broke Me First", which became an international hit and entered the top ten of the Canadian Hot 100 and the top twenty of the American Billboard 100. The debut studio album by Macray I Used to Think I Could Fly took third place in the Canadian music charts and gave rise to the single "She's All I Wanna Be", which entered the top 10. After creating a more pop-oriented sound, her 2023 single "Greedy" was released with global commercial success, taking first place in the Billboard Global 200 and Canadian Hot 100 and third place in the Hot 100.
